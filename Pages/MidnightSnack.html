Midnight Snack


Video Game


14/04/2024


Made with Daniel de linos, Álvaro Lozano and Roberto del Saz


❮❮ BACK ❮❮

The game was developed during the Madrid European University 2024 Game Jam, of which, It won the first prize!

Midnight Snack proposes a creative and weird movement scheme, which becomes the core mechanic you need to master to reach the end. The innovation on the movement combined with a PS1 aesthetic made the game stand out between
its 23 competitors.

For me, someone who is passionate about videogames, to have developed one its a dream come true, and the fact that it came out so good makes me really happy.

You can download and play Midnight Snack on Windows or MacOs in this ❮❮ link ❯❯.
