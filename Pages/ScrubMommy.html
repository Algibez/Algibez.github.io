The Scrub daddy brand wanted new packagings for their products, scurb mommy, premium Wonder Wash up, and their magic multisurface cleaner.

We parted from a way to unify the three packages and looked at the round shape of scrub daddy sponges, our first ideas were similar to cans, however due to dificulties in production we went back to cardboard adapted to the sponge showing the iconic face on the front.

After that we had the bottles selected so we designed the tags and printed them for a proof of concept.

La marca Scrub daddy quería nuevos envases para sus productos, scurb mommy, premium Wonder Wash up, y su limpiador mágico multisuperficies.

Partimos de una forma de unificar los tres envases y nos fijamos en la forma redonda de las esponjas de scrub daddy, nuestras primeras ideas fueron parecidas a latas, sin embargo debido a dificultades en la producción volvimos al cartón adaptado a la esponja mostrando la icónica cara en la parte frontal.

Una vez seleccionadas las botellas, diseñamos las etiquetas y las imprimimos como prueba de concepto.
